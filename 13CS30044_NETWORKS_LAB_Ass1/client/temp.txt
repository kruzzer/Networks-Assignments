HEADLINE : Determine How Long It Will Take to Earn an Undergrad Degree Online

DATE     : Wed Nov 18 01:26:59 2015

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                               NEWS BODY                                       
Prospective students should first check if they already have college credit that can apply to an online bachelor's degree.Figure out if any college credit can be applied toward the degree.Determine what courses need to be completed for the degree program.Decide on how many courses to take per term, also consider the time limit.
